Privacy Policy for Decisio

Last updated: October 2025

Overview

Decisio ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Decisio mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Decision Data: The decisions, criteria, options, and analyses you create within the app
  • App Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app's features

Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Usage Analytics: App performance data and crash reports (if you opt-in)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • Save your decision processes locally on your device
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support when requested

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage (Default): Your decision data is stored locally on your device using Core Data
  • Optional iCloud Sync: If enabled in iOS settings, Apple's iCloud may sync your data via your Apple ID. We do not have access to your iCloud data.
  • Security: We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your decision data remains private and is stored only on your device and/or your iCloud account if you enable sync.

Third-Party Services

We do not include third‑party advertising SDKs. Anonymous crash/performance analytics may be collected only if you opt-in via system prompts.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@decisio.app

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Delete your personal information (by deleting the app)
  • Opt-out of analytics (in app settings)

Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with applicable privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, and other regional privacy regulations.
